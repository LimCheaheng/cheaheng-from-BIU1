Computer Course

Computer course is designed to assist students in information technology in order to keep up with the Digital Age. Computer is one of the most important skills for students to study, to research and especially to perform tasks assigned by lecturers. Computer course lasts for 6-month and which carries 12 credits. The students are allowed to choose a study shift that does not overlap with that of Year 1. Upon successfully fulfilling the course requirements, the university will issue a Certificate of Completion for the students.
Terms and Conditions
1. Candidates of either sex, Cambodian or foreign students, monks, civil servants, company employees and the general public who are pursuing a Bachelor's or Associate Degree in Year 1in the National Program under any Faculty at BELTEI International University can register to study for free.
2. Candidates who are NOT pursuing a Bachelor's or Associate Degree in Year 1 in the National Program under any Faculty at BELTEI International University can also enroll by paying the tuition fee according to the set price list.
3. Students under the International Program or those of Year 2 and Year 3 under the National Program, and whose English proficiency is limited are also eligible to enroll by paying the tuition fee according to the set price list and with the approval of the President and His Excellency Dr., the Chairman.
